How Much Are Quartz Countertops in South Florida

Planning a kitchen or bathroom renovation in South Florida? One of the most common questions we hear is "How much do quartz countertops cost?" The answer depends on several factors, but we're here to give you a clear picture of what to expect.

Average Quartz Countertop Prices in South Florida

In South Florida, quartz countertops typically range from $50 to $150+ per square foot, installed. This price includes the material, fabrication, and professional installation. Here's a general breakdown:

  • Entry-level quartz: $50-70 per square foot
  • Mid-range quartz: $70-100 per square foot
  • Premium/designer quartz: $100-150+ per square foot

Factors That Affect Quartz Countertop Cost

Several factors influence the final price of your quartz countertop project:

  • Brand: Premium brands like Cambria cost more than budget options
  • Color/pattern: Exotic patterns and rare colors command higher prices
  • Edge profile: Simple edges cost less than elaborate profiles
  • Square footage: Larger projects may qualify for volume discounts
  • Complexity: Cutouts for sinks and cooktops add to the cost

Sample Project Costs

To give you a better idea, here are some typical project costs in South Florida:

  • Small bathroom vanity (10-15 sq ft): $500-$1,500
  • Average kitchen (30-50 sq ft): $2,000-$5,000
  • Large kitchen with island (50-80 sq ft): $4,000-$10,000

Why Quartz is Worth the Investment

While quartz may cost more upfront than some alternatives, it offers exceptional value over time. Quartz is non-porous, scratch-resistant, and requires no sealing—perfect for South Florida's humid climate. Plus, quality quartz countertops can last a lifetime with proper care.
