Our Quartz Countertop Services

At Griffin Quartz, we offer comprehensive quartz countertop services tailored to meet your unique needs. From initial consultation to final installation, our expert team ensures a seamless experience.

Free In-Home Consultation

Our design experts visit your home to understand your vision, measure your space with 3D laser technology, and provide personalized recommendations for your quartz countertop project.

Custom Fabrication

We fabricate your quartz countertops in-house using state-of-the-art CNC machinery and hand-polishing techniques for precision cuts and flawless edges.

Professional Installation

Our experienced installers ensure perfect fits with minimal disruption. Most installations are completed in a single day with meticulous attention to detail.

Sink & Fixture Integration

We expertly cut and finish openings for undermount sinks, cooktops, and other fixtures, ensuring seamless integration with your new countertops.
